## Overview
This repository contains the technical write-ups and thought processes for the challenges encountered during the **International Hack@10 CTF 2026**. Representing **Multimedia University (MMU)**, the team successfully qualified as a finalist, competing among the top 25 teams at the Grand Finals in UNITEN Putrajaya.

> "Close enough to taste it, yet far enough to stay hungry."

## Accomplishments
- **Global Reach:** Competed against 111 teams from Malaysia, Indonesia, and Turkmenistan.
- **Finalist Status:** Secured a spot in the Top 20 for the Final Round.
- **Technical Growth:** Gained deep insights into advanced security domains and identified key areas for future specialization.
